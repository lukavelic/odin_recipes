Lasagna recipe

Store bought oven baked lasagna

This is my lasagna recipe that you buy in a store and bake as per the instructions. Should look nothing like the picture.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Store bought lasagna

Steps

  1. Buy the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 200C
  3. Store the ingredients in the fridge for something else and bake the lasagna
